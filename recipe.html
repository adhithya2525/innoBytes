RECIPE

  1. Grate fresh wasabi root using a fine grater or wasabi grater.

  2. Mix the grated wasabi with a small amount of water to form a paste.

  3. Let it rest for a few minutes to release its full flavor and aroma.

  4. Adjust consistency by adding more water if needed, for a smoother paste.

  5. Serve immediately as a condiment with sushi, sashimi, or other dishes.
